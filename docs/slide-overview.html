Objective

Build a complete online cinema platform that enables customers to discover movies, browse showtimes, reserve seats, and pay — all in one seamless digital experience.

The system replaces manual box-office operations with an automated, role-based web application that serves customers, cinema staff, managers, and system administrators from a single unified platform.

Problem Statement
  • 1
    Traditional box-office queues cause long wait times and poor customer experience for moviegoers.
  • 2
    Manual seat management is error-prone, leading to double-bookings and scheduling conflicts.
  • 3
    Cinema staff lack a centralized tool to track payments, check-in tickets, and manage concessions in real time.
  • 4
    No integrated promotion or loyalty system makes it difficult to retain customers and increase revenue.
  • 5
    Administrators struggle to oversee multiple cinema chains, user accounts, and operational data without a unified dashboard.
✨
List of Features
🎬
Movie Discovery
Browse Now Showing & Coming Soon movies with detailed info, trailers, and ratings.
🪑
Seat Selection & Booking
Real-time interactive seat map; customers pick seats and reserve in a step-by-step guided flow.
🍿
Concession Ordering
Add food & drinks alongside ticket booking; browse per-cinema concession menus.
🎁
Promotions & Loyalty
Apply discount codes and accumulate loyalty points redeemable on future purchases.
📲
Ticket Check-In
Staff scan or enter booking codes to validate tickets at the cinema entrance in real time.
💳
Payment Management
Staff process on-site payments and managers review transaction histories and refunds.
🏢
Cinema & Hall Management
Admins manage cinema chains, individual venues, and seating hall configurations.
🗓️
Showtime Scheduling
Create and manage movie showtimes per cinema; handle scheduling conflicts automatically.
👥
Account Management
System admins manage all user accounts, roles, and cinema staff assignments.
🎞️
Movie Management
Add, edit, and remove movies with full metadata: genres, cast, posters, and release dates.
📦
Concession Catalog
Manage concession categories and items; assign different menus per cinema location.
📊
Admin Dashboard
Central overview of bookings, revenue, users, and system activity for managers and admins.
Roles covered: 👤 Customer 🧑‍💼 Cinema Staff 🏛️ Cinema Manager 🔧 System Admin
Tech Stack: ⚛️ React 🍃 Spring Boot 🐬 MySQL 🐳 Docker 🔐 JWT Auth
